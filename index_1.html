Why join

Support Guardian journalism

For 200 years, the Guardian has been fearless and independent. By becoming a member, your financial contribution will help keep it this way, and will support our award-winning journalism.

Recent Guardian initiatives

  • Keep it in the ground

    Join us and more than 226,000 others in urging the world’s two biggest charitable funds to move their money out of fossil fuels

  • End FGM Guardian Global Media Campaign

    The Guardian’s campaign has amplified the grassroots work of campaigners against Female Genital Mutilation in the UK, the US, Kenya and the Gambia

  • NSA files: decoded

    Edward Snowden’s surveillance revelations explained

  • The Counted

    A project by the Guardian – and you – working to count the number of people killed by police and other law enforcement agencies in the United States throughout 2015
